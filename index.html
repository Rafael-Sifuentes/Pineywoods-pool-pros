FAQ's

Have questions ? We have answers.

  • How much do your services cost?

    Pricing depends on your pool's size and service type. Contact us for a free estimate !

  • What is included in a regular pool cleaning?

    Skimming, brushing, vacuuming, cleaning filters, emptying baskets, and balancing chemicals.

  • How often should I clean my pool?

    We recommend weekly cleanings during the swimming season, but it depends on usage and weather.

  • What areas do you service?

    We serve all of Nacogdoches county. That includes Nacogdoches, Garrison, Cushing, Chireno, Appleby, and Redfield

  • How do I know if my pool equipment needs repairs ?

    Signs include unusual noises, leaks, or reduced water circulation. Contact us for an inspection.

  • What types of pools do you service ?

    We service in-ground, chlorine, and saltwater pools. We do Not service above ground pools at this time.

  • Do I need to be home for the service?

    No, as long as we have access to your pool, you don't need to be home.

  • How can I schedule a service?

    Schedule through our email, or by phone at 936.371.6146

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept credit card, debit cards, online payments, or cash
